Our Story

Welcome to Xcell Physiocare

Founded in 2010, Xcell Physiocare has been a trusted name in dental care in New Delhi, Delhi. What started as a small practice has grown into a comprehensive dental care center, serving over 15,000+ patients with dedication and compassion.

Our clinic is equipped with the latest dental technology, from digital X-rays to advanced implant systems. We believe that every patient deserves access to world-class dental care at affordable prices, and that's exactly what we deliver — day after day.

Our team of experienced dentists brings together decades of combined expertise across general dentistry, orthodontics, prosthodontics, and cosmetic procedures. We take pride in our patient-first approach, transparent pricing, and a welcoming atmosphere that puts even the most anxious patients at ease.
